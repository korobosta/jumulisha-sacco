BOSA Loan Information

Kenversity has endeavored to provide financial facilities to its members through various loans. These include Normal loan, Super loan, Emergency loan and School fees loan as shown below:

Normal Loan

This is a loan given 3 times of your deposits. It is repayable in 48months. Interest rate is 12% p.a. at reducing balance. Three (3) guarantors required for Kenyatta University members on permanent employment and 5 guarantors on those on contract and other institutions.

Super Loan

This is a loan given 3 times of your deposits. It is repayable in 60 months. Interest rate is 15% p.a. at reducing balance. Three (3) guarantors required for Kenyatta University members on permanent employment and 5 guarantors on those on contract and other institutions.

Emergency Loan

This is a loan provided for urgent cases e.g. College fee, Medical expenses Funeral. This is a loan given 3 times of your deposits. It is repayable in 12 months. Interest rate is 18% p.a. at reducing balance. Three (3) guarantors required for Kenyatta University members on permanent employment and five (5) guarantors on those on contract and other institutions.

Fahari Loan

Monthly deposits contribution shall be Ksh. 3,000, interest rate 16% per annum, at least five (5) guarantors, repayment period is 72 months, with a maximum of Ksh. 4,000,000 after being a member of the Sacco for at least 6 months. One will be eligible for top up after 12 months. Target group - Platinum A and Platinum B. Salary to be channelled through the Kenversity Sacco Ltd. Processing fee of 2% of the loan shall apply, 1/3 Rule of the Basic salary to apply. New members must retain 10% of the loan as deposits, the loan is not pegged on deposit but on salary

School Fees Loan

This is a loan provided to aid parents/ guardians pay school fees for their dependants. This is a loan given 3 times of your deposits. It is repayable in 12 months (A calendar year). Interest rate is 12% p.a. at reducing balance. Three (3) guarantors required for Kenyatta University members on permanent employment and 5 guarantors on those on contract and other institutions. The appraisal of the loan is based on the school fees structure

Share Booster

To help you get a bigger loan, we increase your deposits by up to 100% of your deposit. The boosting facility will be credited to your member account. This amount will be eligible to earn interest on deposit at the end of the year. Interest charge on boosted amount at 10% (one off).
