Deposits and Savings

At Kenversity SACCO,you can make deposits and save your monney with ease.Money deposited earns an interest rate of 10% per year.There is also an opportunity to open a fixed savings account. Money that is saved under a fixed savings account cannot be withdrawn until the specific period stated is attained.

There is also a goal account where a member saves to reach a certain amount of money.Its only when the goal amount is reached when the member can withdraw the money.This account helps our members to save so that they can do something big with their money.
